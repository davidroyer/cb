FAQ

  • Do you deliver?
    Yes, we deliver to any business or home within a 30 mile radius of Louisville for an additional charge of $10. Delivery of wedding cakes is complimentary.
  • Do you deliver flowers with cakes or supply flowers for wedding cakes?
    No, Christina Bakes does not currently work with a florist. However, florists are accustomed to working with chefs and will set aside a vase of the wedding day flowers if asked ahead of time. Chef Christina will incorporate the flowers into the wedding cake to ensure that it matches the wedding theme.
  • Can you personalize your baked goods?
    Yes, Christina Bakes strives to accommodate all requests for personalized cakes and baked products.
